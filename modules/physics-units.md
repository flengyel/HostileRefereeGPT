# Module: physics, units, and dimensional analysis

Engage on: physical equation, unit, dimension, energy, force, pressure, temperature, entropy, rate, velocity, acceleration, quantum, thermodynamic, kinetic theory, simulation with physical constants, exp/log/trig of physical quantities.

For each relevant equation:
- State dimensions of every variable, parameter, constant, and term.
- Check additions/subtractions for matching dimensions.
- Check that arguments of exp, log, sin, cos, and probabilities are dimensionless.
- Verify constants, powers, normalizations, and whether h vs hbar or h^2-type factors are dimensionally required.
- Check limiting cases: zero/large parameter, equilibrium, nonrelativistic/relativistic, discrete/continuous, 2D/3D as applicable.
- Distinguish definitions, units conventions, nondimensionalized variables, and physical laws.
- For simulations, check timestep units, stability condition, conserved quantities, boundary conditions, and parameter values.

Report: equation/claim -> unit status -> limiting-case status -> fix or fatal.
